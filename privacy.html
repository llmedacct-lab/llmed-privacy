Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the LLMED Menopause CDS WhatsApp service, operated by LLMED LLC. This service provides AI-assisted, evidence-based insights for menopause symptom management — including HRT and non-hormonal options — via WhatsApp. It is for educational use only and is not a substitute for medical advice.

Information We Collect

When you send a message to the LLMED Menopause CDS WhatsApp number, we receive your WhatsApp phone number and the text content of your messages. This is necessary to route your message to the AI reasoning system and deliver a response back to you.

We do not ask for, and you should not send, any personally identifiable information (PHI) or patient-identifying details. All case descriptions submitted to this service should be de-identified before sending.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used only to route the conversation — it is not stored in any database, shared with third parties, or used for marketing. Message content is passed to a third-party AI system (ThunkAI) to generate a clinical reasoning response. We do not retain message content after your session ends. Conversation state is held in temporary server memory and is cleared when the service restarts.

Data Retention

We do not maintain persistent storage of phone numbers or message content. Conversation state is in-memory only and does not survive a service restart. No personal data is written to disk or a database.

Third-Party Services

This service uses the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send and receive messages, and the ThunkAI platform to generate AI responses. Both services process message content as part of delivery and inference. Please review Meta's and ThunkAI's respective privacy policies for information on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

This service is intended for use by healthcare professionals and adult users only. It is not directed at individuals under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this URL. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
